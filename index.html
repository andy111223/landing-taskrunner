About Us

Our mission and story

Cats of Izmir is a volunteer-run organization dedicated to helping the street cats of Izmir, Turkey. Since 2018, we have rescued, treated, and rehomed over 500 cats. Our team of dedicated volunteers works tirelessly to ensure that every cat receives the care and love they deserve.
